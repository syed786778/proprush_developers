Mohammed Baig started Proprush Developers with a simple but powerful belief — every family deserves to own land they can trust, at a price they can afford, in a location that grows with them.

What began as a vision in Mysore has grown into one of South India's most respected land development brands. Over two and a half decades, we have delivered legally compliant, bank-approved plots to over 1,000 families — never once compromising on transparency or legal integrity.
